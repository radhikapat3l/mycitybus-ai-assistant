Project Overview

MyCityBus AI Assistant is an AI-powered transit assistant designed to help Greater Lafayette CityBus riders quickly access information about routes, schedules, fares, accessibility services, and general transit questions using natural language.

The Problem

  • Users had to search through multiple webpages.
  • Finding schedules and fares took time.
  • New riders didn't know where to begin.
  • Important information was scattered across different pages.
